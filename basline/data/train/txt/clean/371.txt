 We all face challenges, big and small, throughout our lives. But sometimes, it can be hard to find the courage to overcome them. That's why it's important to remember that courage isn't just something that you're born with—it's something you can cultivate. And it's the resilience to keep going, even when things get tough. So, the next time you're faced with a challenge, remember that courage is within reach. By taking small steps and pushing yourself, you can build up your courage and find the strength to take on any challenge. Don't be afraid to take risks and try something new—because that's how we learn, grow, and become our best selves.
