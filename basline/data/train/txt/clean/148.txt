 We all have dreams, but it can be hard to turn them into reality. We can get overwhelmed by the enormity of our goals and the amount of work that it takes to get there. But it doesn't have to be so daunting. Instead of focusing on the end goal, focus on the next step. Break your dream down into smaller steps and celebrate each success along the way. By taking it one step at a time, you can make progress towards your dream without feeling overwhelmed. So don't be afraid to dream big, and take it one step at a time.
