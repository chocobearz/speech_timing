However, the greatest joy often comes from the small acts of kindness we show to others. Whether it’s a smile, a hug, or a kind word, these gestures can have a profound effect on both the giver and the receiver. They can make us feel connected to each other, and remind us of the beauty of life.

So, I encourage you all to take a few moments each day to find joy in the little things. You don’t have to do anything big or expensive.
